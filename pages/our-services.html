Our Services

We offer global yachting assistance for cradle to grave requriements, from booking your berth and clearing you and crew to stocking your fridge and importing engine spares. Our full-service solutions include Berthing, Immigration & Customs, Provisioning & Catering service, Fuel, Event Reservations and much more. Please see below for a full list.

Anchorage

Waypoint manages your berth bookings and marina reservations for any destination. You choose it, we arrange it. We offer an unrivalled service that will take the tedium out of planning an escape.

Immigration & Customs

We liaise with Customs and Immigration Offices to ensure that all issues such as customs clearance, crew immigration and yacht charter regulations are taken care of. Services also support ship spares import and yacht customs advice.

Provisioning & Catering services

Our yacht provisioning service guarantees the finest dishes on your table by providing the freshest local produce and sourcing all the specialist products you may require. Enjoy quality yacht provisioning and yacht catering especially designed for a gourmet clientele.

Bunkering & Fuel

Waypoint supplies bunker fuels, lubricants and related products and services for your yacht worldwide.

Cleaning Services

All your interior needs are seamlessly taken care of by our team, who know what it means to go the extra mile. Yacht toiletries, ecological yacht cleaning products, laundry services, dry cleaning or anything else, we are always by your side.

Other Assistance

As a global agency Waypoint will ensure safe and efficient reception and delivery of all courier needs and storage.

Reservations & Activities

As a total concierge agent, Waypoint can bridge the gap between a trip to a total escape! We can organize an island excursion, reserve event tickets, dinner reservations, chauffer hire, or deck the bridge in roses for your anniversary. If you wish to be hands-off, we can be hands on.
