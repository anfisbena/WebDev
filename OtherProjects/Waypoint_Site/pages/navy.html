Our Services

In Waypoint LLC. we are specialists providing logistics services for the Department of Defense. We have three lines of business. The first is focused on the maintenance and service of ships and personnel. The second is groceries and delicatessen items. Our latest line of business is logistical support for high-impact, diplomatic, rescue missions, etc.

LLC is a long serving husbanding support provider (HSP) for wide ranging categories if vessels including US Navy and Coast Guard worldwide.

Waypoint also specializes in first-class and effective deliveries of food provisions and supplies to ships in upon their arrival at port. In cooperation with major suppliers worldwide, we can supply a wide range of fresh products fully stocked for their next voyage.

Serving the US and the world is our purpose as a company. Waypoint LLC provides the necessary logistics support for all Military Departments and Services of the Department of Defense of the US to fulfill the execution of expeditionary missions throughout the world.
